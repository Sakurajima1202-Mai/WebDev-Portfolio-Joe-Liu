NO POVERTY

"Eradicating poverty in all its forms" is the first of the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals. It aims to completely eradicate extreme poverty worldwide by 2030 and alleviate relative poverty for all, ensuring that everyone has access to basic living conditions and social security.

IMPORTANCE

Ending poverty is the first and most urgent global goal because it is the foundation for human dignity, equality, and sustainable development. Without access to basic needs like food, shelter, healthcare, and education, people cannot thrive. By eradicating poverty, we break cycles of inequality, promote social stability, and create opportunities for everyone to live a safe, healthy, and fulfilling life.
